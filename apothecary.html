Behind The Apron

Apothecary + Alchemy

Recipes, dinner party menus, and the stories behind the meals that brought people together. A former chef's kitchen — preserved for the next generation.

Before the boardroom, there was the kitchen. Before leadership development, there was mise en place. The discipline, creativity, and hospitality that shaped my career started with a cutting board and a cast-iron pan.

This is my culinary vault — a collection of recipes, dinner party menus, and the stories that go with them. Some are scribbled on index cards. Some are screenshots from group texts. Some live only in my memory (for now). All of them are being gathered here so Parker, Mackenzie, and every generation after them can cook what I cooked, host like I hosted, and keep bringing people to the table.
