Questions

What happens after I sign up?

I'll send you a TestFlight invite (iPhone) or a Google Play beta link (Android) as soon as each app you picked is ready. Some are live now, others are coming soon. You'll be first in line either way.

What do I actually have to do?

Use the app. Break things. Tell me what's weird, what's great, and what made you want to throw your phone. There's a dedicated feedback page that makes it easy to report bugs or share ideas.

What's in it for me?

One full year of premium access on every app you test, completely free. No credit card. No catch. I just need honest feedback from people I trust.

Can I sign up for more than one app?

Absolutely. Tap as many as you want. The more you test, the more free premium you get. You'll get 1 year of premium on each app you help with.

I found a bug. Now what?

Head to the feedback page and tell me about it. Takes 30 seconds. I read every single one and usually fix things same-day.
